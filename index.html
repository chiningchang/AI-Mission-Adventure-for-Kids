Temperature

Temperature controls how creative AI becomes.

  • Low temperature: safer and more predictable.
  • High temperature: more creative, but sometimes sillier.

AI Vocabulary Card

Bias

Bias means favoring one idea or group over another.

AI learns from human data. If the data contains unfair patterns, AI may learn them too.
